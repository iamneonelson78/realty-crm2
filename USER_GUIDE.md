# Realty CRM - Official User Guide

Welcome to the Realty CRM! This platform is designed specifically for modern real estate professionals. It offers a clean, efficient, and robust set of tools to manage your leads, track property listings, automate social media post generation, and connect with clients across multiple communication channels.

This guide is divided into two main sections:
1. **Agent Guide:** For real estate agents managing their daily workflows.
2. **Administrator Guide:** For platform administrators managing users and system features.

---

## Part 1: Agent Guide

As an Agent, your primary workspace revolves around managing your pipeline, handling property listings, and communicating effectively with potential buyers or renters.

### 1.1 Getting Started & Account Setup

- **Sign Up / Login:** Navigate to the main application URL. You can sign in using your provided credentials. If you are a new user, you must register and await Administrator approval before gaining access.
- **Profile Management:**
  - Click on your profile icon in the top right corner.
  - From the sliding profile panel, you can update your **Full Name**, **Phone Number**, **Location**, and **Avatar**.
  - You can also switch to the "Change Password" tab to update your security credentials.

### 1.2 Dashboard & Overview

The Dashboard is your command center.
- **Metrics at a Glance:** Instantly view your total leads, active listings, and conversion rates.
- **Recent Activity:** Quickly access leads that need immediate attention or have been recently updated.
- **Dark Mode:** Toggle the moon/sun icon in the top navigation bar to switch between Light and Dark viewing modes based on your preference.

### 1.3 Pipeline Management (Kanban Board)

The Pipeline view allows you to visualize your sales process.
- **Lead Stages:** Leads are categorized into stages: *Inquiry*, *Contacted*, *Viewing*, *Reserved*, and *Closed*.
- **Drag-and-Drop:** Easily click and drag a lead card from one stage to another as their status progresses.
- **Lead Details:** Click on any lead to view or edit their contact information, specific requirements, and any notes you have recorded.

### 1.4 Listing Manager & FB Post Generator

The Listing Manager helps you keep track of your properties and quickly market them on social media.
- **Adding a Property:** Enter the Property Title, Location, Monthly Rent, Bedrooms, and any specific rules (e.g., "No Pets, 1 month advance").
- **FB Post Generator:**
  - Select a property and click "Generate Post".
  - Choose from built-in templates (e.g., "Modern Highlight", "Friendly Agent") or create your own **Custom Templates**.
  - The generator automatically replaces placeholders (like `{{title}}` or `{{rent}}`) with your property's details.
  - Click "Copy to Clipboard" to instantly paste the formatted text into Facebook or Messenger.

### 1.5 Connections

The Connections feature links your CRM to external communication channels.
- **Adding Channels:** You can link your Messenger, WhatsApp, Viber, or Facebook handles.
- **Benefits:** Once connected, your "FB Post Generator" will automatically embed your specific contact links into your posts, ensuring leads know exactly how to reach you with one tap.

## Part 2: Administrator Guide

As an Administrator, you have access to a specialized "Access Control" dashboard. You are responsible for maintaining the security and configuration of the platform.

### 2.1 Access Control Dashboard

Navigate to the **Admin** tab to view the comprehensive user table.
- **Search & Filter:** Use the search bar to find users by name, email, or location. Use the filter chips (Pending, Active, Suspended) to quickly segment users.
- **Pending Approvals:** A banner will notify you if there are new signup requests waiting for review.

### 2.2 User Management Actions

For any user in the system, you can perform several critical actions directly from the table:

- **Approve / Suspend / Reject:**
  - **Approve** new users to grant them access to the platform. An automatic welcome email will be sent out.
  - **Suspend** active users to temporarily revoke their access.
  - **Reject** pending signups if they are unauthorized.
- **Role Management:** Use the dropdown menu in the "Role" column to promote an Agent to an Admin, or demote an Admin to an Agent.
- **Security & Passwords:**
  - **Reset Email:** Send a standard password reset link to the user's email address.
  - **Temporary Password:** Generate a secure, temporary password (e.g., `Corevia-x82j9d!`). The password will be copied to your clipboard to send to the user manually. The user will be forced to change this password upon their next login.
- **Delete User:** Permanently remove a user from the database. *Note: This action cannot be undone.*

### 2.3 Feature Toggles

Administrators can control which features are available to specific users.
- **Connections Toggle:** In the "Features" column, click the switch icon to enable or disable the "Connections" module for an individual agent. If disabled, the agent will see a locked screen when attempting to access the feature.
